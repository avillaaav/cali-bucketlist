California Places to Visit

Beaches in California

  • Malibu Beach

    Located in Malibu, California, this beach is known for its excellent surfing conditions and breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean. The beach also has several amenities such as showers, restrooms, and picnic areas. Parking is available for a fee.

    Service hours: Sunrise to sunset

    Adress: Malibu, CA 90265

    More information: Malibu Activities

  • Santa Monica Beach

    Santa Monica Beach is one of the most famous beaches in California, located in the heart of Santa Monica. The beach offers a variety of activities such as swimming, surfing, and beach volleyball, and is adjacent to the Santa Monica Pier, which has an amusement park and an aquarium. Parking is available for a fee.

    Service hours: Sunrise to sunset

    Address: Santa Monica, CA 90401

    More information: Santa Monica Things to Do

  • Newport Beach

    Newport Beach is a popular destination for beachgoers, located in Orange County. The beach offers a variety of activities such as swimming, surfing, and sunbathing, and is known for its picturesque scenery. The beach also has several amenities such as showers, restrooms, and picnic areas.Parking is available for a fee.

    Service hours: Sunrise to sunset

    Address: 70 Newport Pier, Newport Beach, CA 92663
