California Places to Visit

Theme Parks in California

  • Disneyland

    Disneyland is a world-famous theme park located in Anaheim, California. It is a magical place where dreams come true, featuring thrilling rides, enchanting shows, and beloved Disney characters. With its iconic castle, immersive lands, and unforgettable experiences, Disneyland is a must-visit destination for Disney fans and families alike.

    Service hours: Varies by day and season

    Address: 1313 Disneyland Dr, Anaheim, CA 92802

    More information: Disneyland Official Website

  • Universal Studios Hollywood

    Universal Studios Hollywood is a world-renowned theme park and studio tour located in Universal City, California. It offers thrilling rides, shows, and attractions based on popular movies and TV shows, such as the Wizarding World of Harry Potter, Jurassic World, and The Simpsons. Visitors can also take the famous Studio Tour to see behind-the-scenes of movie and TV production. Parking is available for a fee.

    Service hours: Varies by day and season

    Address: 100 Universal City Plaza, Universal City, CA 91608

    More information: Universal Studios Hollywood Official Website

  • Knott's Berry Farm

    Knott's Berry Farm is a popular amusement park located in Buena Park, California. It offers a wide range of thrilling rides, family-friendly attractions, live shows, and themed areas, including Camp Snoopy for younger children and Ghost Town for a taste of the Old West. The park is also known for its delicious boysenberry treats and jams, which originated from the original Knott's Berry Farm fruit stand. Parking is available for a fee.

    Service hours: Varies by day and season

    Address: 8039 Beach Blvd, Buena Park, CA 90620

    More information: Knott's Berry Farm Official Website
